Trust nobody

A weekend of secrets, strategy and suspicion

House of Deceit is a hosted, two-night social game played inside a private manor house in Whitstable. Guests take part in challenges, secret missions, murders and dramatic trials while trying to discover who among them can be trusted.

Immersive

The story unfolds around you throughout the weekend. You are not watching the mystery—you are inside it.

Social

Designed for people who enjoy games, conversation, performance and meeting a house full of suspicious new allies.

Hosted

The experience, accommodation and scheduled activities are organised as one complete weekend.
