What

A seaside mini-break with a twist

Bring your friends together under one roof for a two-night immersive game of secrets, strategy and suspicion.

Hidden killers will begin picking off your group one by one. Everyone else must work out who can be trusted before it is too late.

The experience is designed to keep the whole group involved throughout the weekend, leading to a dramatic finale at 9pm on the second night—when you discover which of your friends has been deceiving you all along.

Who

Who is it for?

Everyone is welcome. House of Deceit is ideal for birthdays, friends’ weekends, reunions, hen and stag parties, and groups looking for something genuinely different.

It also works brilliantly for team socials, company off-sites, away days and client entertainment. If your group enjoys social strategy games, murder mysteries or television shows built around trust and deception, this is for you.

How

How to book

1. See the dates

Start the booking process to view the weekends currently available.

2. Choose a weekend

Agree on a date with your group. Standard groups are 10–15 people.

3. Secure the date

The lead booker pays £166, covering their own place.

4. Bring in the group

The remaining minimum balance must be paid within 14 days.

5. Choose how to pay

Friends can pay their own shares, or the lead booker can pay for several people.

Groups of 16–20 must enquire before booking because sleeping arrangements become considerably cosier.

Included

Food and drink

The weekend is self-catered. The house has a large kitchen with five ovens and two full-size fridges, and supermarket deliveries can be sent directly to the property.

Welcome pizzas from the Woodfired Kitchen are included on the first evening.

Fully catered options may be available by prior arrangement. Email house@houseofdeceit.com.

Where

Whitstable, Kent

House of Deceit takes place at 59 Northwood Road, Whitstable, Kent, CT5 2HA.

There is ample street parking nearby. The house is approximately a 15-minute walk from Whitstable railway station. Guests receive a detailed arrival guide before the weekend.
