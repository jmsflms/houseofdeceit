A weekend of secrets, strategy and suspicion

House of Deceit is a hosted, two-night social game played inside a private manor house in Whitstable. Guests take part in challenges, secret missions, murders and dramatic trials while trying to discover who among them can be trusted.

Immersive

The story unfolds around you throughout the weekend. You are not watching the mystery—you are inside it.

Social

Designed for people who enjoy games, conversation, performance and meeting a house full of suspicious new allies.

Hosted

The experience, accommodation and scheduled activities are organised as one complete weekend.

Customer information

Booking policies

Cancellation and refunds

Deposits secure a booking once received and matched. Full cancellation, transfer and refund terms will be supplied with the booking details before the remaining balance is due.

Problems and disputes

Customers will be asked to contact us first at house@houseofdeceit.com. We will investigate booking or payment problems promptly and respond in writing.

Privacy

We only use information submitted by email to respond to the enquiry and administer future bookings. We do not sell personal information. Marketing emails will only be sent where a person has expressly requested them, and they may unsubscribe at any time.

Service description

House of Deceit sells admission to hosted immersive entertainment weekends in Whitstable, England. The precise accommodation, activities, schedule, inclusions and restrictions will be provided for each advertised date before purchase.
